Our Products and Services

Passenger Elevators

Smooth and efficient vertical transportation designed for residential and commercial buildings with customizable designs and advanced safety features.

  • Load capacity: 4 to 20 persons
  • Speed: Up to 2.0 m/s
  • Customizable cabin dimensions
  • Advanced safety mechanisms

Hospital Elevators

Specially designed for medical facilities, accommodating stretchers and medical equipment with precise alignment and voice announcements.

  • Load capacity: 1000 kg to 1500 kg
  • Wide entrances for stretchers
  • Micro-leveling for precise alignment
  • Voice announcement system

Goods Elevators

Built for heavy-duty use in industrial and commercial applications, capable of handling heavy loads with energy-saving modes.

  • Load capacity: 2000 kg to 2500 kg
  • Wide cabin dimensions
  • Durable construction
  • Energy-saving mode

Home Elevators

Compact and stylish elevators designed for residential use, offering convenience and accessibility in your home.

  • Space-saving design
  • Customizable interiors
  • Quiet and smooth operation
  • Energy-efficient technology

Hydraulic Elevators

Reliable and robust elevators powered by hydraulic systems, ideal for low-rise buildings and heavy-duty applications.

  • High load capacity
  • Durable hydraulic mechanism
  • Smooth start and stop
  • Low maintenance requirements
